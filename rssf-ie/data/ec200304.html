Note: in this season, extra time was played according to the so-called silver goal rule (meaning that if a team is in the over-all lead after the first period of extra time, the match is finished; the away goals rule did not apply in extra time); this rule was first applied in the 2003 UEFA Cup final between Celtic and Porto). Rule abolished after one season.
