Introduction

The European Cups Archive contains the complete results of all the main European club competitions since the inception of the Champions' Cup in 1955 to the current season.

The archive can also be found at James Ross' Own Pages.

A searchable version of the archive constructed by Milos Radulovic is available at http://alas.matf.bg.ac.yu/~mg94a14/ec.html. All enquiries about Milos's search engine should be directed to him, although I would be interested to hear of any errors as the data is taken directly from this site.
