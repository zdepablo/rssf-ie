Champions' League Consists of six groups of four clubs playing each other home and away. Three points are awarded for a win, one point for a draw. Teams level on points are seperated by aggregate score (including away goals) in games against each other, and then overall goal difference in all group matches. The group winners and the two best number two qualify for the Quarter-Finals.
