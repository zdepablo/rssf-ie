UEFA Champions' League (Sep 16, Sep 30, Oct 21, Nov 4, Nov 25, Dec 9) Consists of six groups of four clubs playing each other home and away. Three points are awarded for a win, one point for a draw. Teams level on points are seperated by aggregate score (including away goals) in games against each other, and then overall goal difference in all group matches. The top team in each group qualifies for the Quarter-Finals along with the two best runners-up. Team shown capitalised in red qualify for the Quarter-Finals.
